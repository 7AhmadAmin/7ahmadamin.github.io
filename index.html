about

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate and Research Assistant at the South China University of Technology (SCUT), Guangzhou, China, in the Research Centre of Multimedia Information Security Detection and Intelligent Processing led by Professor Yongjian Hu since 2016. Prior to my Ph.D., I received my M.Eng. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from the School of Electronics and Information Engineering @ SCUT in 2018, advised by Professor Yongjian Hu also, and won the best M.Eng. Dissertation and Excellent Graduate Student's Awards from the School of Electronics and Information Engineering and School of International Education.

During my Ph.D., I have worked on multiple industrial collaborative research and development projects with GRG Banking LTD. as an Algorithm Engineer at R & D Institute. Further, on several research-based projects, I collaborated with Professor Chang-Tsun Li's Lab at the School of Info. Technology, Deakin University, Australia. Also, hosted by Professor Yu Guan at the Department of Computer Science, University of Warwick, England, and Professor Jiankun Hu at the School of Systems and Computing, University of New South Wales, Australian Defence Force Academy, Australia.

I am passionate about exploring various research areas, including:
  • Biometrics and Forensics: Continual Learning for Domain Generalization, Multimedia Deepfakes Detection
  • Image/Audio/Video Processing: Representation Learning, Multimodal Learning
  • Learning with Real-world Data: Un-/Self-/Semi-/Supervised Learning

    • In order to make a significant impact on human lives, my long-term research goal is to develop advanced embodied AI systems utilizing multiple agents and modalities (such as language, vision, and audio) across diverse research fields. In particular, I have dedicated my efforts towards addressing practical challenges in application domains that span various disciplines, such as multimedia forensics, information security, image/video processing, pattern recognition, machine learning, and computer vision.
